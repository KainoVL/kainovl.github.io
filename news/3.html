Kaino v2.0.0 - The Rewrite

Released on July 27, 2024 01:41 UTC

We are thrilled to announce the launch of Kaino Version 2.0.0, representing months of dedicated development and marking our most significant update to date..

Key Enhancements:

  1. We have achieved 100% compatibility with the Roblox External Naming Convention (RENC), complementing our existing 100% compliance with the Unified Naming Convention (UNC) and our 100% implementation of Vulnerability Mitigations, further solidifying our commitment to user safety and easy scripting.
  2. After sessions after sessions of testing and refinement, we have successfully implemented Multi-Inject functionality, expanding Kaino's abilities. Forget Wave, Kaino is the future.
  3. We've addressed and resolved previous detection issues, ensuring Kaino maintains undetected.
  4. Kaino now offers full support for a wide range of obfuscation methods, including but not limited to Luarmor, MoonSec, and Comet.
  5. Our team has completely rewritten the injector, optimizing its performance and reliability.

This release represents a significant milestone in Kaino's development, reflecting our ongoing commitment to innovation, security, and user experience. We extend our gratitude to our dedicated community for their continued support and feedback throughout this process.
