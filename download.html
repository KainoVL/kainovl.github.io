Kaino isn't out yet.

So then when?

Soon™ (3-5 business days)

In all seriousness, we aren't going to give an actual release date. Kaino will be ready when it is ready. Giving a release date puts pressure on developers, and you don't know if something will happen between the time we announce the release date and the day of the announced release date. We don't want to end up like Wave, where we announce a release date and then go, "oh guys we've got to delay because: blah blah blah" We're doing everything we can to make Kaino the best it can be while bringing it to you as quickly as possible.
